About Me

Austin Simons is a certified Human Resources professional with over three years of progressive experience in HR Operations. Currently serving as a Human Resources Generalist at Avance Care in Durham, NC, Austin specializes in comprehensive talent management, from onboarding and offboarding processes to HRIS administration, HR analytics, and dashboard development. Austin combines technical proficiency with strategic HR thinking. He has successfully organized employee engagement committees, streamlined onboarding processes, and developed data-driven reporting systems to support organizational decision-making.
