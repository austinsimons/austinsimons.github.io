About Me

Austin Simons is an American producer, writer, director, and actor. He was born and raised in Greensboro, North Carolina, and found a fascination with film from a young age. As he began to attend North Carolina State University, he began to explore this passion further through the university's film studies program. He is solely responsible for the short film, The Toymaker (2020), in which he performed every aspect of it's production completely by himself.
